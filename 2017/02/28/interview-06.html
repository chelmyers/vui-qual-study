Memos

The user crafts very complicated ways to say phrases. This might be because they are uncomfortable with the task and haven’t done it with voice control or with other programs. This may be their way to think while saying.

User has not done a lot of these tasks outside voice control himself. He is not as confident with his phrases and has the most different responses we have seen so far.

The two people that want more than personal assistant tasks with voice control don’t use personal assistant aids whatsoever themselves.

How to Use Cychic

  • Tap "" to add a marker.
  • Select the type of spot in the pop up and hit save.
  • A color coded marker will pop up at your current location.
  • Color Code:
    • Metered
    • Metered with Lock
    • Alley or Sidewalk
    • Alley or Sidewalk with Lock
    • Parking Lot
    • No Type
  • Remove a marker by tapping it.

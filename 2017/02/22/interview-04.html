Memos

User gets very frustrated when the VUI gives too much information and he cannot stop it.

So far, every interviewee has said task 1 the same way.

When a command is overheard, the user may repeat is blindly even though he is not familiar with the outcome.

User misinterpreted the flash briefly, was impatient, and stopped it all together. He assumed Alexa was more intelligent that is was and misheard his command, but was reading out menu options. Instead, Alexa was reading out the Flash Briefing (triggered by news phrase). Was this a mistake of Alexa or was the user not listening due to interview pressure?

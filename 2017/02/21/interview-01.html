Memos

User distrust the accuracy of VUI. They generally believe they have a low accuracy. This is based on their experience and assume it applies to all other users.

People don’t know what they can say and it takes too long for them to look it up. They rather just use a GUI.

Phrases might be biases off use of Siri.

To-do list is used over grocery list. Is grocery list an unknown feature of Alexa? Do they use to-do list because it something they use already.

User could has just decided to repeat the prompt for task 3.

Perhaps TMI is related to habits. The baseline information is what they are used too. TMI occurs when it is more, or too different that their habit.

After news report is too long with task 4, user think that is their fault and they should have been more specific.
